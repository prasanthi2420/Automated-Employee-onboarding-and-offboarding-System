# Automated-Employee-onboarding-and-offboarding-System

The Automated Employee Onboarding and Offboarding System is a comprehensive web-based HR management solution designed to streamline and automate the entire employee lifecycle, from joining an organization to their final exit. The system eliminates manual and paper-based processes by providing a centralized platform for managing employee information, document verification, workflow approvals, asset allocation, and access control. During onboarding, it simplifies employee registration, digital document submission, profile creation, department and role assignment, onboarding task management, and automated approval workflows, ensuring that new employees are integrated quickly and efficiently. During offboarding, the system manages resignation requests, exit approvals, clearance processes, company asset returns, account deactivation, and final documentation, ensuring a secure and well-organized transition. Built with a focus on security, scalability, and usability, the application incorporates role-based authentication, centralized data management, automated notifications, and real-time status tracking to improve collaboration between HR, managers, IT teams, and employees. By reducing repetitive administrative tasks, minimizing human errors, improving compliance, and enhancing transparency across HR operations, the system delivers a seamless digital experience that increases organizational productivity and provides a reliable, efficient, and user-friendly solution for modern employee lifecycle management.
